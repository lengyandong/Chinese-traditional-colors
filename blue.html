Chinese traditional colors

blue

Blue is very pure. It usually reminds people of the ocean, the sky, the lake and the universe.

Bi Luo（碧落）

Taoism in ancient China called the first layer of the east sky, blue clouds full of sky, called "Bi Luo". Later, it refers to the sky.

Cloisonne（景泰蓝）

The color of Cloisonne. It is one of the famous special metal handicrafts in China. In the Jingtai（景泰） period of Ming Dynasty, this kind of technology reached its peak, and the handicrafts produced were the most exquisite and famous. Therefore, this kind of metal ware was later called "Cloisonne".

The color of a special kind of Azurite. People in the Ming Dynasty in China use it as a kind of blue paint.

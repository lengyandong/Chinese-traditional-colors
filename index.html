Chinese traditional colors

China is one of the first nations in the world to know how to use color, and the concept of positive five colors(positive five colors [in Chinese:正五色],means yellow[黄], cyan[青], red[赤], white[白], and black[黑]) appeared during the Warring States Period.








Why I made this website?

I watched the Spring Festival Gala of China. One of the program (below the paragraph)showed the beautiful traditional colors (and its poetic names), which impressed me a lot. Then I made this repl to show these colors.

 

What can the website do?

You can learn about Chinese color culture. Also, you can use the colors to make your website more beautiful.
